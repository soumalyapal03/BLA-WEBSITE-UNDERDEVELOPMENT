About Us

Aikyataan - The Bengali Literary Association Of Vellore Institute Of Technology Is One Of The Most Esteemed And Prestigious Literary Associations Known For Its Plethora Of Multilingual Events Bringing Up The Bengali Culture. It Is A Culmination Of All Bengalis In VIT Vellore Campus. We Engage And Indulge In Various Events Of Our Culture As Well All Inter Cultural Events. Our Aim Is To Provide A Fulfilling Experience For Everyone Eager To Join Us And Make Every Moment Memorable. Our Club Provides You Opportunities To Enjoy As Well As To Spend Every Event With Pomp And Splendour. So Come Join Us And Immerse Yourself In The Rich Cultural Aspects Offer You.
